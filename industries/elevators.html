BIS & NBC Standards CPP Encodes

IS 17900 became the operative BIS standard on 22 December 2025, withdrawing IS 14665, IS 15785, and IS 14671. CPP constraint graphs are built against the current standard — and flagged for update whenever BIS revises.

BIS
IS 17900 Part 1 & 2 — 2022
Operative from 22 Dec 2025 · Replaces IS 14665
  • →UCMP — Unintended Car Movement Protection: CPP locks drive and brake spec to prevent car motion with doors open
  • →ACOP — Ascending Car Overspeed Protection: speed selection drives safety gear type constraint
  • →Refuge spaces — Pit ≥ 0.5 × 0.6 × 1.0 m; car top ≥ 0.5 × 0.6 × 1.0 m: pit depth and headroom selections validate automatically
  • →Aligned with ISO 8100-1 and ISO 8100-2 for international export projects
NBC
NBC 2016 — Part 8, Section 5
National Building Code · Lift & Escalator Installation
  • →Building ≥ 15 m mandates dedicated fire lift — CPP triggers fire-lift BOM variant automatically
  • →Fire lift spec — rated load ≥ 545 kg, speed ≥ 1 m/s, 1-hour fire resistance: locked as constraints when fire-lift type selected
  • →Fireman's switch and EMT recall panel — BOM line items enforced at building-height selection
  • →Electricity Act 2003 backup power requirements encoded for DG set spec
BIS
IS 14665 Parts 1–4 (Withdrawn Dec 2025)
Legacy reference for existing installed base
  • ·Part 1 — Outline dimensions for passenger lifts ≤ 2.5 m/s
  • ·Part 2 — Installation, operation, maintenance; car/counterweight clearances
  • ·Part 3 — Safety rules: min car areas per rated load, overload protection
  • ·Part 4 — Buffers, guide rails, machines, brakes, wire rope spec
  • ⚠Superseded — CPP migrates legacy constraint graphs to IS 17900 automatically

European Standards — EN 81 Series

Full EN 81 Coverage in CPP

Indian manufacturers exporting to Europe, or supplying multinational customers, must comply with the EN 81 series. CPP encodes both BIS and EN 81 rules in the same constraint graph — the engine flags BIS-only versus dual-compliance at configuration time.

EN 81-20 : 2014
Safety Rules — Construction & Installation
  • → Replaces EN 81-1 (traction) and EN 81-2 (hydraulic)
  • → Car area ↔ rated load relationship table
  • → Min pit depth & headroom vs. rated speed
  • → Door protection resolution ≥ 50 mm
  • → Fire-resistant shaft & car materials
  • → Progressive safety gear: v ≥ 1.0 m/s
EN 81-50 : 2014
Design Rules — Safety Components
  • → Testing & certification of safety gears
  • → Overspeed governor type vs. rated speed
  • → Buffer type (energy-accumulation vs. dissipation)
  • → Parachute/safety gear gripping force calc
  • → Complements EN 81-20 for component BOM
EN 81-21 : 2018
New Lifts in Existing Buildings
  • → Reduced pit depth solutions (fold-down buffer)
  • → Reduced overhead / top clearance solutions
  • → Constraints adapt to available shaft height
  • → Common in modernisation and retro-fit projects
EN 81-28
Remote Alarm Systems
  • → Two-way voice communication mandatory
  • → Alarm panel BOM line items enforced
  • → 72-hour emergency battery backup spec
EN 81-70
Accessibility — Persons with Disabilities
  • → Min car dimensions: 1100 × 1400 mm
  • → Door clear opening ≥ 800 mm
  • → Landing buttons height 900–1100 mm
  • → Braille + tactile indicators on BOM
EN 81-76 : 2025 (New)
Evacuation Lifts
  • → First EU standard for disability evacuation by lift
  • → Supersedes "never use lifts in fire" instruction
  • → Fireman's recall + evacuation control modes
  • → CPP flags evacuation-rated BOM package
EN 81-71
Vandal-Resistant Lifts
  • → Reinforced car enclosure grades (VR1 / VR2)
  • → Anti-vandal door skins and button panels
  • → Material & panel spec locked per vandal grade
EN 81-77
Seismic Conditions
  • → Seismic detector and car stop spec
  • → Guide rail reinforcement per seismic zone
  • → Applicable for Indian seismic zones III–V (IS 1893)
EN 81-41
Vertical Platform Lifts (VPL)
  • → Low-rise accessibility platforms (≤ 3 m travel)
  • → Speed ≤ 0.15 m/s, rated load 250–500 kg
  • → Separate BOM tree from passenger lifts

How Standards Become Constraints

Each standard clause becomes a named constraint rule in CPP. When a sales engineer selects a speed, load or building type, the engine propagates all downstream implications in milliseconds — no manual cross-check.

Standard Clause / Rule CPP Constraint Action
IS 17900 UCMP — door open + car moving Drive type + brake spec locked; safety relay BOM line added
IS 17900 ACOP — ascending overspeed protection Bi-directional governor mandated when v ≥ 0.8 m/s
IS 17900 Pit refuge ≥ 0.5 × 0.6 × 1.0 m Pit depth selection validates min 1,600 mm total
NBC 2016 Building ≥ 15 m → fire lift mandatory Building height input triggers fire-lift BOM variant auto-selection
NBC 2016 Fire lift rated load ≥ 545 kg, v ≥ 1 m/s Fire lift type locks min load + speed; EMT panel BOM added
EN 81-20 Car area ↔ rated load table (cl. 8.2.3) Load selection forces max permissible car area; invalid combos blocked
EN 81-20 Door protection resolution ≥ 50 mm Door sensor spec locked per speed class
EN 81-50 Buffer type: energy accumulation v ≤ 1.0 m/s Speed > 1.0 m/s forces energy-dissipation buffer on BOM
EN 81-70 Accessible car: min 1100 × 1400 mm Accessibility flag expands min car dimension constraints + Braille panel
EN 81-77 Seismic zone installation IS 1893 zone input → guide rail reinforcement & seismic detector BOM
IS 17515 Energy performance label (5-star to 1-star) Drive regeneration option affects BEE label calc — shown at selection

Elevator types CPP supports

Residential & Commercial

Standard Traction Lifts

IS 17900-compliant BOM for 6–24-person cars. Dealer-configurable without engineering review. UCMP, ACOP, refuge spaces validated at selection. BOM derives in <3 seconds.

High-Speed / High-Rise

Speeds ≥ 2.5 m/s

Progressive safety gear (EN 81-50), bi-directional governor, energy-dissipation buffers and machine room constraints fire automatically. No manual cross-reference of IS 17900 vs. EN 81-20.

Goods & Freight

Rated Load ≤ 5,000 kg

Special platform and door configurations (bi-parting, up-and-over). CPP enforces structural, drive and guide rail interdependencies per IS 17900 Part 2 freight-specific clauses.

Home Lifts

IS 15259 — ≤ 5 Floors

Compact, wall-hugging configurations with limited pit and overhead. CPP enforces IS 15259 travel limit, rated load ≤ 400 kg and speed ≤ 0.4 m/s constraints.

Accessibility

EN 81-70 / IS 15330

Wheelchair-accessible cars (min 1100 × 1400 mm), door clear opening ≥ 800 mm, Braille panels, tactile indicators — all added to BOM when accessibility flag is set.

Fire & Evacuation

NBC 2016 + EN 81-76

Fire lift BOM enforces ≥ 545 kg load, ≥ 1 m/s speed, 1-hour fire rating, EMT panel and fireman's switch. EN 81-76 evacuation mode package auto-selects for export projects.
